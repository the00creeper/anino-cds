> [!quote] AniNews
> ### Anino Founder announces new DONATOR rank; reveals updates for future events
> ANINO, Philippines — Anino SMP founder Soul Baron announced the creation of the DONATOR rank this Thursday midnight on AninoSMP stage, as Discord server boosters peak at 28/28.
> 
> According to Baron, the BOOSTER role will be unavailable until slots are freed if server boosts expire by the end of the month, unless renewed, which paves the way for players to avail the new DONATOR role.
> 
> The DONATOR role will be released by the time its exclusive permissions are deliberated by server staff.
> 
> Higher DONATOR ranks, such as DONATOR+ and DONATOR++, are still under consideration.
> 
> Contrary to the BOOSTER role, which expires by the end of the month unless renewed, the DONATOR role will be permanent and is being planned to start at $9.99 (₱580).
> 
> The cooldown on teleport commands for the DONATOR was also revealed to be five to three minutes, a step up from the BOOSTER rank’s eight-minute queue.
> 
> Baron also addressed recent server issues, including the diminishing spawn rates, which he confirmed will be returned to normal soon.
> 
> The server’s TPS (ticks per second) has also been reportedly stabilized, which has been causing noticeable server lag for days.
> 
> The founder also opened the suggestion submissions during the meeting, to which he approved and confirmed the possibility of custom images on walls and player heads, but denied the following: cosmetics, custom pets and clothes, backpack mod, the /back command, jobs plugin, and the controversial Jenny Mod.
> 
> Baron also mentioned that the official announcement for the upcoming building contest, in which the prestigious BUILDER role will be granted, will be announced on September 28th.
> 
> He also revealed the ongoing construction of a “coliseum” somewhere in the server and opened the possibility for a PvP event soon.
> 
> Baron encouraged the players to participate in server events, as he would be giving away physical equipment as rewards, namely mice, headsets, keyboards, and even premium Minecraft accounts from Microsoft.
