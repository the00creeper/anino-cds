# ZynxIPChecker 1.2a Updated to ZynxPlayerInfo 1.2b
## What’s New?
- Updated command `/wholeinfo <player>` – shows complete player information:
	- Name
	- Date Joined
	- Last Seen
	- Playtime
	- Latest IP and country lookup.
- Lists other accounts that have used the same IP with first-used timestamp
## Why It Was Necessary / Benefits:
Helps admins quickly gather complete player information, including IP history and shared IP accounts –- crucial for moderation and detecting potential alternate accounts.
