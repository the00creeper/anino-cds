> [!quote] AniNews
> ### Players discover exploit to reveal base locations; permanent ban issued
> 夢・Shinjiru, AniNews
> Published Sep 27, 2025 03:41 AM PHT
> 
> Anino, PHILIPPINES — Players have discovered an exploit yesterday evening that reveals the last logout location, and potentially the bases of other players.
> 
> The exploit begins by logging in to the server with a cracked account and using the username of the targeted player; the exploiter then gets registered as the targeted player and joins the server at their last logout location.
> 
> The coordinates are revealed by opening the F3 menu using the targeted player’s account without ever having to know their password.
> 
> Conflicts arose yesterday evening after players KnightKing and raxergames11 raided the base of players Zaide, MENGOMEN, and others despite being thousands of blocks away from spawn.
> 
> Server logs from the console revealed that KnightKing and raxergames11 logged in to Zaide and MENGOMEN’s accounts which exposed their location to the two attackers, who were later banned for “hacking.”
> 
> It was only earlier tonight when KnightKing appealed and debunked the server logs, claiming that it was Zaide who attempted to login using his account.
> 
> According to him, “KnightKing” was only a nickname he set to conceal his real username “SoulessBaron,” which then tricked Zaide to log in using “KnightKing” as username, therefore creating a fresh Minecraft account that has never logged in to the server before.
> 
> SoulessBaron was later unbanned upon a more deliberate examination of the console logs, proving that it was only raxergames11 who carried out the exploit, therefore sustaining the verdict of ban on him.
> 
> Zaide clarified earlier that he attempted to access SoulessBaron’s account out of anger after a long history of conflicts between them, especially in the admin abuse incident tied to SoulessBaron, who was then known as “Tai_” when he was still part of the server staff.
>  
> Anino Admin and Developer Aulsie released an apology after the incident in the [Hall of Shame](https://discord.com/channels/1409166097885827144/1409232600089886871/1421176252966109347), believing he had jumped to conclusions “way too fast” and held SoulessBaron accountable upon taking only a brief glance at the console logs.
> 
> The developers have now patched this exploit and players will now join at spawn instead of their last logout location upon login to enter their password.
