- Created the logo graphic icon for the server's news program *AniNews*.
- This was made using joint-software collaboration with Blockbench, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe Photoshop. 
- The base of the logo used Blockbench's Minecraft title generator.
- The superimposition of the letter N to form the combined two N's of *AniNews* was also done on Blockbench. 
- The exported logo was taken to Photoshop for the refinement of edges.
- Having an official logo gives the Minecraft news program a sense of professionalism. 
- The official logo can now be used in the program's print and video news formats.
