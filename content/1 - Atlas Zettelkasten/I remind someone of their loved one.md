 The old lady next door that is mama’s ninang saw me once again while waiting for a ride to [[Nueva Ecija University of Science and Technology|Sumacab.]]
 
She told me once again that I remind her of her grandson who committed suicide last year.

I had a previous encounter with her back then and what reminded her of grandson in me was the CSHS ID lace I wore and my Chinese-collared polo uniform.

I infomed her that I am now a college student at NEUST in Cabanatuan; she replied that her grandson would've also been college and an NEUSTian if he were still here.

Being the memento of someone that is long gone and is loved is a reason to keep going.
