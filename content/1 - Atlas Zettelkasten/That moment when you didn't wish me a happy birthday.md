That moment when you didn't wish me a happy birthday was the end of everything for me.

Then I dreamed about you sa simbahan. I saw you and [[Jemimah Relucio|Jem]] first gaya ng dun sa Wesleyan. I ignored you guys on purpose but you approached me, apologizing. The moment that dream ended, so did everything else about you.
