She guessed my favourite color on the first try, but between you and me, I didn’t even have a favourite color until she yelled out “**yellow**!”

She was excited and smiling like a little kid, so I told her she was right, and I haven’t seen yellow the same since—it’s in everything, I could probably live in it now.

She is the most beautiful pattern of beauty on the fabric of love. She is a poem, and a painting too. Everything she said sounded like a song, and every silence was music. 

She is an old soul with young eyes, a vintage heart, and a beautiful mind.
