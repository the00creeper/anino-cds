[[Entablado]], Philippines — A long conversation had sparked between long-time exes Pierre and Gwyneth last night—a conversation that had been cooking for **two years**. 

It began when her boyfriend, Edren, **confronted** Pierre on Messenger about him following Gwyneth back on TikTok, saying that whatever happened between them must stop since it had been long gone. This is because Gwyneth recently began looking at Pierre’s socials, suggesting that something must have gone **wrong** in their relationship.

Gwyneth shockingly revealed that before she even started looking at Pierre’s socials (notably Instagram and TikTok), she had **already** broken up with her boyfriend, sparking a splendor of hope in Pierre’s eyes.

Pierre showed pictures of Gwyneth hurting herself on her wrist, which he added that she was not so happy in their relationship, with Edren being **possessive**.

Gwyneth mentioned that the last time she saw Pierre was with [[Hanna Mae Garcia|Hanna]] at **[[San Isidro]]**, together in a single, around May 2024. *Shortly after Pierre uttered Hanna’s name, Hanna came **cruising along** the street, probably on her way home.*

They got to know each other’s recent lives. They also talked via Messenger call. From the way Pierre described her replies, she talked like **Mariz**, always joking sarcastically even on the most serious topics.

What made me frown though is when Pierre informed Gwyneth about what happened between me and Mariz, in which she replied, **“*Hindi naman na ako nagulat.*”** It feels as if it has been long **obvious** that we were not meant to be. Are we really that far apart to last **forever**?
