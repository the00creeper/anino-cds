To my enthusiastic high schooler self,

I don't even know who you are anymore. You used to be someone totally different, yet what remains of you is the collective remnant of your former splendor. 

What the hell happened?

I'm starting to miss that weird, albeit true smile.

I'm starting to miss that smile you used to wear, the very smile that was yet to understand reality and the pain it brings.

Scott Street
