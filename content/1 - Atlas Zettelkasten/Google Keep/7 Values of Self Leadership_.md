1. Serve others before yourself, tao muna bago sarili dahil sinumpaan mo ang katungkulang ito, hindi ka pinilit na maging leader kusa kang sumali
2. How to inspire vision? — be sure to share not only the “what” and “how” of your vision but also the “why.”
3. Choose ethics (before profit) — serbisyo muna bago kita. Unahin natin kung ano maitutulong natin kaysa sa kung ano ang makukuha natin pabalik
4. Empower others (before personal gain) — to make them realize that they can, dahil kapag nag-empower o nagpakilos o nagpa-realize ka ng ibang tao, they can also empower more people
5. Priviledge people (before tasking them) — make sure to appreciate even the smallest efforts of your team, it inspires them, it gives them morale. Look at them as colleagues, not as employees.
6. Balance focus and flexibility (before making decisions) — accept change and innovation, lahat ng bagay nagbabago at kailangan nating mag-adapt sa pagbabago
7. Serve with humility — be humble, “Humility is not thinking less of yourself, it's thinking of yourself less.”

“An effective leader is a man of humility.”
