
We are one step closer to reaching our dreams, and for each step we take, we grow. Although there are a still a lot more steps TO BE taken, let us look at how many steps we have ALREADY taken; you might even realize that, “Wow, ang layo na ng narating ko, ang dami na ng pinagdaanan ko, at heto na ako ngayon, look at me now.”

The lesson to be taken here is always look at the goodness in everything. Gaya nga po ng sinabi ko kanina, “There's always something good for every bad situation,” you just have to open, not your eyes, but your heart to see it clearly.

The COVID-19 pandemic was really an invisible disaster. It took a huge toll on the country's economy and marami po sa ating mga kapwa at mahal sa buhay ang kinuha ng sakit po na ito.

Minsan lang tayo bata. Sa pagtanda natin, hahanap-hanapin natin ang mga araw na tayo'y mga bata pa lamang.
