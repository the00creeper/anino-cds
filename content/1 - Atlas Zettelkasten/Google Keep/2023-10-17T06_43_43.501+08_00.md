Revenge is the action of inflicting hurt or harm on someone motivated by getting a satisfaction for the wrongdoings they had done to you. It is a really common trait in people and I do think it is an unpleasant and toxic mindset. Patience is a person's capacity to wait, or to continue doing something despite difficulties, or to suffer without getting mad or becoming annoyed. Anger on the other hand, is a strong feeling of annoyance or rage because of something wrong or bad. It's the feeling that makes someone want to hurt other people, to shout. 
Rony V. Diaz's "The Centipede" conveys the connection between vengeance, patience, and anger, and how they can influence the relationship between people.
