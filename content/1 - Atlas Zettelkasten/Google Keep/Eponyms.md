An **eponym** is a person, place, or thing after which something else is named, or is believed to be named.

Examples:
- **America**: named after Italian explorer Amerigo Vespucci.
- **Fahrenheit**: named after the physicist Daniel Gabriel Fahrenheit.
- **Diesel engine**: named after Rudolf Diesel.
- **Caesar Salad**: named after its creator restauranteur Caesar Cardini.
- **Boycott**: named for an Irish land agent Captain Charles C. Boycott
- **Sandwich**: named for Earl of Sandwich—a town in England.
- **Nicotine**: named after Jean Nicot who sent powdered tobacco leaves and seeds back to France when he visited Portugal as an ambassador.
