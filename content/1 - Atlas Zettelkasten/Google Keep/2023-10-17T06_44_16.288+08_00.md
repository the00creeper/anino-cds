
It's already past 4 in the afternoon and Kizurid is still at the park. She's been trying to help the cat get down from the maple tree for almost 30 minutes now but just couldn't reach it. She'll definitely be late for her job interview at this state. She just couldn't resist cats. Then, just as she was about to reach the poor kitty, the mood suddenly changed. The atmosphere felt heavier and had drastically been filled with despair. She felt something eerie. As if somebody was watching her. She look around her and everything was monochrome. She looked up into the depths of the sky and saw something really disturbing that it shook down through her whole spine. Something Kizurid and probably no one had ever seen before. It is truly and literally out-of-this-world.
