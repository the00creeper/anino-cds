
Now I'd like to share you a little secret. I found it in a book that an important person of mine made me read. “It is only with the heart that one can see rightly; what is essential is invisible to the eye.” Hindi lahat ng ginto, mata ang nakakakitaaaa. A gold miner might suspect na yung pader na tinititigan niya eh wala nang ginto base lamang sa nakikita niya, hinusgahan lamang niya ito gamit ang mga mata niya. When in fact, pag ginamitan niya ng makabagong teknolohiya, gaya ng (halimbawa) metal detector, dun lang niya malalaman ang kayamanang nakatago sa isang pader na minsan niyang inakalang pader lang. Don't judge the book by its cover; read the book and then discover. Hindi kasi lahat ng nakikita natin eh yun yan. It is only with the heart that one can see rightly. It is the time you spent on your plant that made your plant special from other plants. It is the love you gave to your cats or dogs that made them different from other animals. It is the laughter we shared with our friends that made them true. All of which could only be seen with the heart.
