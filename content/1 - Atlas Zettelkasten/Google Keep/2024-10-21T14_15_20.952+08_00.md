
The University Social Weather Agency (USWA) has released the official survey results regarding the most popular internet activities done by students of Wesleyan University-Philippines this October.

According to the USWA survey, with around 1,200 student-participants, 79% of students spend most of their time doing personal or professional emails, followed by 70% through visiting websites for information and online shopping.

The lowest among the most popular activities was for playing online video games at only 10%, followed by downloading or streaming movies online at 25%.
