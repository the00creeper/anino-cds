All your emotions are valid, but your **behaviour** is not.

Your anger is valid, but your **yelling** is not.
Your jealousy is valid, but your **judgment**  is not.
Your pain is valid, but your **drinking** is not.
You missing them is valid, but **going back** to them is not.

**Separate** your emotions from you behaviour. Emotions are always valid, no matter what your feel, but behaviour is not.
