# Insights 
- Emphasized that you should be building your folders from the bottom up, gathering notes first and creating relevant folders as you go.
## PARA Method 
An organizing system designed by [[Tiago Forte]].
	**1 - Projects**
		Notes that get updated in status through time; projects.
	**2 - Areas**
		Notes related to fields of interest.
	**3 - Resources**
		Notes about consumed content, concepts, or thoughts.
	**4 - Archives**
		Storage of files.
PARA does not house
## ACE Method
A very concise and minimalistic organizing system.
	**1 - Atlas**
		Thinking, ideas, knowledge
	**2 - Calendar**
		Time-based; temporal notes
	**3 - Efforts**
		Action-based; projects
