## SEP 15
Centralized document system
- all minutes, agendas, proposals, announcements as documents (duly signed)
- babase sa journal channel (react ako ng check if na-document na) at sa excel completed tasks ng bawat member
- these documents be stored and organized in a google drive file
- appointment of secretary(ies) must be held

Division of Departments
- Department of Moderation and Administration (Admins, Mods, H. Mods)
- Department of Media and Communications (Media, may create more subroles soon) 
- Department of Server Development (Developers)
- Rationale behind this is so that the staff may perform meetings specific to their roles
- Therefore, there should be channels (voice and text) for these departments as well

Types of Meetings
- Department-wide meetings (D.DOMC Meeting)
- General Meetings (G. Meeting)
  - Agenda (drafted by admins)
    - Topic Rationale
      - Inquiry of rebuttal
    - 
  - Proposals

Planning of meetings (process)
- Initiated by a head, or by majority of the department out of immediate necessity 
- Must have a specified agenda
- A poll is performed to determine the date and time of the meeting
- The agenda is forwarded to the secretary
- The finished agenda document is sent to the #upcoming-meetings (proposed) channel for notice of the statt

- as much as possible, we must avoid spontaneous meetings
- for all suggestions, a poll must be created to decide for approval, with a minimum of 50% among present staff
