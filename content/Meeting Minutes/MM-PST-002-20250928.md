# Minutes of the Meeting
## Building Contest
### Criteria for Judging
1. **Judges’ Score – 80%**
	- a.  Creativity & Visual Design — 30%
		- Creative use of blocks and materials.
		- Overall beauty and style.
		- Good details (lighting, landscaping, decoration).
	- b.  Relevance to Theme (Culture) — 30%
		- Shows cultural elements (architecture, art, symbols).
		- Fits the theme clearly.
		- Shows respect and understanding of the culture.
	- c.  Originality — 20%
		- Idea is unique and fresh.
		- Stands out from other entries.
		- Not copied from common builds.
2. **Audience Factor (Tiktok Engagement) – 20%**
	- Number of likes and comments (agreements of the viewers)
	- Engaging explanation of the build.
	- Strong positive reaction from viewers.
### Bonus Points
Functionality & Playability — up to +5%
- Build is interactive or usable.
- Easy to explore and navigate.
- Has practical or fun features (like redstone).
