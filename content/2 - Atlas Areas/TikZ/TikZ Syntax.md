# Preamble
Commands in TikZ must be inside a code block consisting of three opening backticks (` ```tikz`)  and three closing backticks to work. 
# Draw
[[2025年03月10日 (Mon)]]
- `\draw (x1,y1) -- (x2,y2);` 
	- Draws a line from ($x_{1},y_{1}$) to ($x_{2},y_{2}$).
- `\draw (x1,y1) rectangle (x2,y2);`
	- Draws a square/rectangle from corner ($x_{1},y_{1}$) to corner ($x_{2},y_{2}$).
- `\draw (x,y) circle (r);`
	- Draws a circle centered at ($x,y$) with radius $r$.
