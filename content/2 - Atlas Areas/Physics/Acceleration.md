# Fundamental Equations
$$
\begin{aligned}
a&=\frac{v-v_{0}}{t} \\[1.25ex]
v&=v_{0}+at \\[1.25ex]
x-x_{0}&=\left( \frac{v_{}+v_{0}}{2} \right)t \\[1.25ex]
x-x_{0}&=v_{o}t+\frac{1}{2}at^2 \\[1.25ex]
x-x_{0}&=\frac{v^2-v_{o}^2}{2a}
\end{aligned}
$$
